Recipe of the Week

Grilled Sausage Delight

A perfect recipe to make your sausages shine

Ingredients

  • 4 premium sausages
  • 2 bell peppers, sliced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • Olive oil and herbs

Prep Time: 10 min | Cook Time: 15 min

Instructions

  1. 1 Preheat grill to medium-high heat
  2. 2 Brush sausages and vegetables with olive oil
  3. 3 Grill for 6-8 minutes per side until golden
  4. 4 Serve hot with your favorite sides
